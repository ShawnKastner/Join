What is Join ?

Join is a Project Management tool to create tasks, assign colleagues and have them be visible for all Members of your Team!

How to use it?

1.

Firstly you must sign in and create an account to create tasks. Alternatively you may use the guest account.

2.

Create tasks and edit them, when a task reaches the next step to completion move the task cards to the adjacent slot to signal your team progress!

3.

Manage and overlook your assigned tasks and stay updated!
